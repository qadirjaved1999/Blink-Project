Long Haul Road Freight

How it Works:

After the creation of the Blink Freight account, drivers will search for shipping loads to be sent by potential clients. The Blink App will allow you to sort for available loads by a variety of criteria including the following; Trailer type, Location, Pick-up date, Drop-off date, Short regional, or long distances.

Once the load is identified, the User is able to request for the job based on a given pricing proposed by the driver.

Subsequently, the User/Customer would then make a choice based on the delivery details provided and the response from the driver.

If the User/Customer feel that the details provided, including the pricing is appropriate for such a shipping load, the will book that driver with the specified truck.

The App will automatically send a notification to both parties confirming the task.

For future, the customer can set up a regular notification for regular pick up with the standard specification which drivers would respond to.

Customers will be able to select drivers based on their ratings. Bookings can therefore be with drivers with high ratings rather than with a poor track record.

Blink Long Haul Freight makes the booking process quick, easy, and transparent by the Drivers showing upfront pricing and allowing the customers to instantly book jobs, Cargo Agents, Retailers, Wholesalers can make educated decisions as to which jobs they should take.

The Blink App will navigate the driver to the desired location for pick up and later drop off. Once the drop is made - the app will allow for Proof of Delivery (POD) to be generated by the recipient which will reflect in the Blink app at the pick-up location. This will also enable for payment to be made with the agreed designated time line by the customer.
