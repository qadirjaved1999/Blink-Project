Air Freight & Transport

How it Works:

The Retailer/Wholesaler/Small Medium Enterprise would use the mobile application to initiate a 'Cargo/Parcel pick up'.

Nearest suitable 'Last Mile Delivery' driver would provide suitable pricing for pick up and delivery to destination.

The app will also identify suitable cargo agents providing end destination pricing linked to a variety of cargo airlines.
